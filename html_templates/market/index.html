Why Choose Us

More than just a market

We bring together the best local farmers, craftsmen, and food artisans to create a sustainable and thriving community hub.

Farm Fresh

Produce harvested within 24 hours of market day. Taste the difference of truly fresh, seasonal fruits and vegetables.

Support Local

100% of our vendors are independent businesses from within a 50-mile radius. Keep your dollars in the community.

Sustainable

Committed to zero-waste practices. We encourage reusable bags, composting on-site, and sustainable packaging.
